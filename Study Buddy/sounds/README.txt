STUDY BUDDY - SOUND ASSET REQUIREMENTS

This document outlines the required sound assets for the Study Buddy Chrome extension.
All sound effects should match the 16-bit retro aesthetic of the extension.

REQUIRED SOUNDS:

1. tick.mp3
   - Short, subtle tick sound that plays every minute during focus sessions
   - Should be reminiscent of digital watches or 16-bit game timers
   - Duration: Very short (< 0.5 seconds)
   - Volume: Subtle, not distracting

2. alarm.mp3
   - Completed session notification sound
   - 16-bit style jingle or alert
   - Duration: 2-3 seconds
   - Volume: Noticeable but not jarring

STYLE GUIDELINES:
- Use 8-bit or 16-bit synthesized sounds
- Low bit-depth, characteristic of retro games
- Clean waveforms without modern processing effects
- Simple composition with limited frequency range
- Avoid high-fidelity or realistic sound recordings

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
- Format: MP3 (for compatibility)
- Sample Rate: 44.1 kHz
- Bit Rate: 128-192 kbps
- Channels: Mono or Stereo

SOURCES:
- Create using chiptune or 8-bit sound generators
- Modify existing royalty-free retro sound effects
- Use tools like BFXR, SFXR, or similar 8-bit sound effect generators
- Ensure all sounds are royalty-free and properly licensed for commercial use
